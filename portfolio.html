My Work

Below are examples of the work that I have done for some of my favorite clients.




Melissa Chin Gallery - Custom Landing Page

Melissa Chin Gallery requested custom art work, as well as a form to gather customer names and email addresses. I created this custom work of modern art using a combination of HTML and CSS.


You can learn more about this project here.


Melissa Chin Zoo - Animal Biography Page

Melissa Chin Zoo requested a page that introduced their animals to new customers. I spent a week at the zoo getting to know the animals so that I could write bios that captured their unique personalities. I also took the photos!


You can learn more about this project here.


Melissa Chin Grocery Services - Grocery List Design

Melissa Chin Grocery Sevices came to me looking for a new design layout for their grocery list app. My design allows customers to create their grocery lists in an environment that is colorful and fun. Say goodbye to the days of using a boring pencil and paper!
